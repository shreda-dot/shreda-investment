Awsome Services

At SHREDA, we offer a wide range of shredding services to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our services include on-site shredding, off-site shredding, and hard drive destruction. We understand that every client has unique requirements, and we work closely with you to develop a customized shredding plan that fits your needs.

On-Site (Mobile) Shredding

A shredding truck equipped with industrial-grade shredders comes directly to your home or business location. You can often witness your documents being shredded on the spot, providing maximum security and peace of mind, with top security not been compromised.

Off-Site Shredding

Documents are securely collected from your location and transported to a secure shredding facility where they are destroyed. This is often a more cost-effective option for large volumes of paper, as the shredding company can optimize their operations at their dedicated facility.

Scheduled Shredding

For businesses that regularly generate sensitive documents, shredding companies provide secure, lockable bins or consoles. These are picked up on a pre-determined schedule ensuring ongoing compliance without the need for internal shredding.

One-Time Purge/Bulk Shredding

Ideal for large clean-outs, office moves, or annual record purges, this service allows you to dispose of a significant volume of accumulated documents in a single, secure shredding event. which wont take up to a long time. trust the process!!!!

Hard Drive & Electronic Media Destruction

Beyond paper, shredding companies often offer secure destruction services for electronic media, including hard drives, solid-state drives (SSDs), backup tapes, CDs, DVDs, and other data-bearing devices.

Certificate of Destruction

A crucial service that provides documented proof that your confidential materials have been securely destroyed in compliance with relevant regulations (e.g., HIPAA, GDPR). This certificate is important for audit trails and demonstrating due diligence.
